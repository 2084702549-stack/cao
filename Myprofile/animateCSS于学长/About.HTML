ABOUT PAGE

Welcome users to the cross-cultural online assistance webpage. We mainly serve international students, tourists and business foreigners from various countries. Regarding this website, we have designed the following relevant contents:

① Translation and Beginner Guidance: We provide you with the location you want to visit and the business you need to handle. We offer real-time translation and guidance for you.
② Product identification: We need your permission to use the camera to scan the product and search for similar products on the web page.
③ Community: We offer a community to all users. The community is a platform for everyone to evaluate various local scenic spots, visits, hotels and other physical stores, and share experiences, helping others who are not familiar with the local situation.
